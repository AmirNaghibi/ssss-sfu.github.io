Frosh

What is Frosh?

Frosh is the freshmen introduction week for University students. The school and your faculty will be hosting events, introductions and workshops to welcome you to your university. This is a chance to meet classmates, seniors, students from your faculty, and professors and staff crucial to your student success.

Your Frosh

The SSSS Frosh will introduce you to your program and university, exploring student life, courses, and campus, both at Surrey and Burnaby. You will meet and collaborate with fellow freshmen in the SSSS, as well those from the Mechatronic Systems Engineering Student Society (MSESS), along with students from the Computer Science Student Society (CSSS) and the Engineering Science Student Society (ESSS). You will learn tips and tricks to succeeding in Software Systems and SFU, ask seniors for course advice and environment setup, and compete and represent the SSSS in a number of events across campus.
