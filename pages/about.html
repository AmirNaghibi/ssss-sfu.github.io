About Us

Our Culture

We are Board Game Masters, Smash Champions, Coffee Addicts and Experts at our Passions. We can compete at a National level and our ideas have spread across the world. We keep a tightly knit community, while kind and open. We will argue with you with strict facts and concrete logic, give strong criticism and motivate you to grow and improve all in the same day.

Our Office

Our office, SRYC Room 4016, is a small gathering place for people from all across SFU. It's doors are always open, so swing by and say hello! We have a couch, a mini fridge, and many office supplies, so if you need to borrow some markers, or want to play smash melee against the best of the best, we've got you!

Get Involved

How can I get Involved?

The Software Systems Students Society is always looking for help!
There are plenty of opportunities to get involved in the society. The easiest way is to join our discord and message an executive!

Executive Positions

The Executive Team is responsible for carrying out the mission of the SSSS, and is a year long role.

Non Executive Positions

Project Developer - Do you have a project that would benefit members of the SSSS? Take on a project on your own schedule with the help of the SSSS.

Event Organizer - Do you want to help organize an SSSS event, but don't want to join as an executive? Event organizers work on specific events, helping the executive team in various ways.

Event Volunteer - Do you want to help out at an event only on the day of? Event Volunteers help out for a certain set of time on the day of the event, and is a great way to dip your toes into working for the society.

Web Developer - Do you want to empower the society through technology? Web Developers create solutions and tackles problems on an issues basis.
