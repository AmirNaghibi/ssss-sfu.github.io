SSSS Hackathon

SSSS's Annual Hackathon draws in over 200 university students from across British Columbia. We design our experiance to empower beginner programmers, or even those with no coding experiance at all. Our team of mentors provide hands on learning, and our event coordinators focus on creating an inclusive environment for failure, experimentation, socialization and creating great ideas.

Unique Features

Our hackathon is uniquely beginner focused. From our themes to our volunteers, every mentor, every scheduling detail, every decision we make is in pursuit of creating the best experiance for a beginner to programming and hackathons. Mentors come to you, food and snacks are plentiful, and our staff are always encouraging students and engaging with their ideas. Our goal is to open the gates and push students to explore and discover new passions, interests and potentials.
