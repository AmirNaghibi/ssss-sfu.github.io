About Us

The Software Systems Student Society (SSSS) is comprised of Software Systems Students and the Executive Team

The Executive Team

The Executive Team directs, plans, and coordinates operational activities for the SSSS, devise policies and strategies to achieve our mission. The Executives represent the Students at meetings, conferences, and around our community.

Executives are elected through a yearly election, in which any Software Systems Student may join. Learn more about each role and our constitution here.
