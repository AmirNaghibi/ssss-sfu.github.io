Hackademia

What is Hackademia?

Hackademia is a week long coding competition where participants learn the necessary skills via an online instructional at the start of the week, followed by a week of coding a project following the theme and the instructional skills. The project is presented during a community night showcase, with prizes given to the instructor and community picks.

July Hackademia

We debutted Hackademia as an alternative to a Hackathon due to Covid, where the event would stetch over a week to accomodate online teamwork, and with the addition of an instructional to offset having in person mentors. The event was hosted completely on our discord, with the instructonal on Basic Web Development and the theme being Outer Space.
