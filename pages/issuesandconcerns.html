Throughout your university career, you may run into issues or concerns about your experience at SFU. It is important to know that regardless of the issueâ€™s scope or topic, your experience matters.

It matters not only because you deserve a good university experience, but also because your fellow students, your friends, and the many who come after you deserve a good university experience. But what can you do?

No matter the scope, or when it took place, if you have or have had a problem as a student, tell us about it. If this doesn't work with you, reach out at ss-pres@sfu.ca or contact the President on our discord.

We will do our best to work together to solve these issues with you, and equally as important, PREVENT such issues from arising again. This is why our form matters.
