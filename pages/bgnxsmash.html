BGNxSMASH

Board Games Night X Smash

Each month at SFU Surrey, we gather to play Board Games and Smash Melee and Ultimate. Food, drinks and games are all provided, but feel free to bring your own! Each alternating month we switch hosts between MSESS and SSSS, and we love to collaborate with other SFU clubs, such as the Surrey Campus Committee or SFU Dank Memes Gang.

Smash

The SSSS hosts Super Smash Bros. as a main game in our monthly Board Games Nights. We have both Melee and Ultimate setups along with controllers and we have hosted tournaments varying between 1 vs 1's, doubles, and crew battles against other faculties. People of any skill level are welcome to join and attend!
